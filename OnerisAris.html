Onaris Aris

Onaris Aris is a level based on an ice covered moon of Jupiter. The player ends up on the moon through a crash landing. Through exploration of the surroundings they discover an abandoned building in the distance, not of human origin. The project was started in Unreal Engine 3 but has since then moved to Unreal Engine 4.

  1. 1. Made this area narrow to prevent player from seeing the rest of the level. Blizzard conditions will also pick up after the player crosses narrow pass. This will further prevent the player from seeing things far in the distance and will make them explore things in the immediate area that may seem out of place.
  2. 2. First area of interest, the abandoned outpost. The player will look around for clues to explain what happened here. Maybe this outpost belonged to the inhabitants of this planet, if so what happened to them? The block-out currently has a temporary house asset to hold the place of the camp that we envisioned for this outpost.
  3. 3. There is to be a fork in the path here. The player will be given the option of choosing which path they would like to take. Both paths will eventually lead to the same place, but each will have a different experience.
  4. 4. The player would then take a bridge that would lead them into the building, but the bridge would collapse halfway and they would fall onto some piping which also led to the building. After following the piping they would find an alternate entrance which would lead them into the maintenance area.

After crossing the pipe-bridge, the player will come to a decontamination chamber that leads to a maintenance tunnel. The tunnel should give a few hints about the kind of security this facility once had, and the condition of the facility now. The power is out, and the player must follow emergency lights that turn on once the door opens. The emergency lights will lead to a generator, and unlock doors to progress further. The lighting will cast large shadows, once turned on, and the hallways should feel cramped, until the player reaches a wide open stairwell that leads up to the bright upper entryway. Looking over the rails, however, will reveal a deep, dark pit. We want the player to ask themselves â€œJust how deep does the facility go?

This part of the level was blocked out in Unreal Engine 4 unlike the exterior. The player will emerge from the side of the room into a large atrium which will have a large focal piece in the center. Behind it will stairs that lead up to the second floor where all the laboratories are located. After the stairs the player will see a number of doors, most of which lead to different labs. The player will have the chance to explore the different labs and see what the inhabitants of the facility were working on all the while looking for more clues to explain what happened.
