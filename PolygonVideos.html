Videos

For this video I was capturing Call of Duty 4 Modern Warefare for the Museum of Play's preservation project. Whenever I captured gameplay I always made sure to capture something unique to that game but I only had 15 minutes to capture because after that the file size would be massive. Therefore I decided that I wanted to capture a ground mission and a vehicle mission. I found that I could do this since there were two missions back to back which met the criteria but it would take too long to play through both. I decided to start the ground mission halfway and play through it as quickly as I could and then play through as much of the vehicle mission as well. This took me a few tries because of how the AI reacted slightly differently each time.

This is a stop motion animation that I made for a college project. I decided that I wanted to keep my animtion video game themed and therefore picked two classic games and figured out a way to put them together. I took about 200-300 shots and then edited them together to create the final project. I was really happy with how it came out and ended up being one of the few in the class that decided to do stop motion. Also being able to eat your work after you're done is always a plus.
